Raising Publicity Through Brand Identity: BIA Website Redesign

THE BRIEF

Defining Brain Injury

“Brain injury is damage to living brain tissue resulting from an internal or external injury or event.”

How might we improve the UI of and brand identity of BIA’s website to see increased publicity and event popularity?

THE PROBELM

Benefits of an enhanced IA

Although we assumed the main benefit of the website for the organization was donations, the stakeholder clarified that only 7k of a $5.5million budget comes from donations.

If we redesigned the information architecture and the nav bar, users would find the volunteer signup and sponsorship pages more easily.

THE USER

Security and Legitimacy

100% of users expressed interest in verifying the ligitimacy of an organization before getting involved.

If we don’t prioritize 501(c)(3) information and the Board of Directors, users may feel uneasy about supporting BIA.

THE SOLUTION

Strengthening Brand Identity

While reviewing the previous site, users stated that the site should look more “clean and polished” in order to be useful.

Along with streamlining process flows related to verifying legitimacy, we created a design system to bring an aesthetic and minimalist design to the site.
